The Lord Of The Rings Movies

Directed by Sir Peter Jackson

The Fellowship of the Ring

This is the first movie in the fantasy Lord of the Rings trilogy, and follows the journey of Frodo to destroy the one Ring.

The Two Towers

This is the second movie in the trilogy, and continues with following the now broken up Fellowship and their own seperate journeys, all focused on one thing: To destroy Sauron and his Ring.

The Return of the King

The long-awaited finale of the trilogy. The Return of the King will keep you on the edge of your seat as tensions rise and armies clash. Who will win the battle for Middle Earth?
